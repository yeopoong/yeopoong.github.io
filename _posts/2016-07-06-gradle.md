설치 및 환경설정 
---------------

### Download

>$ wget https://services.gradle.org/distributions/gradle-2.14-bin.zip  
>$ unzip gradle-2.14-bin.zip 

### Env

```
GRADLE_HOME=C:\dev\gradle-2.14
PATH=%GRADLE_HOME%\bin
```
